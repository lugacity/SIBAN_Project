SIBAN ECOSYSTEM

Immerse yourself in the dynamic atmosphere, discover the latest trends, and witness the collaborative spirit driving blockchain adoption in Nigeria.

SWIPEB

Just like every global organization passionate about women's empowerment, SiBAN maintains a Women in Blockchain Empowerment Program, called SWiBEP, which is focused on women's inclusivity. SWiBEP is tasked with promoting women's growth and development in the Nigerian blockchain space.

BACPAC

Every serious organization needs a formidable community to thrive. SiBAN operates the Blockchain and Crypto Platforms and Channels (BACPAC) arm, a community extension of SiBAN. BACPAC sees to the effective management of SiBAN's channels and platforms, including LinkedIn, Telegram, Twitter, and WhatsApp. It actively engages with channels and platforms across the blockchain and crypto communities, involving corporate and non-corporate members to generate value for players, partners, and sponsors.

BEACON

The Blockchain Empowerment and Charity Outreach Nationwide (BEACON) is the charity arm of SiBAN. BEACON gives the blockchain industry a human face and a human heart by identifying, designing, and supporting SiBANâ€™s charity projects. It partners with brands within and outside the blockchain industry to promote corporate social responsibility (CSR) and use Web3 as a tool for socio-economic impacts.

BACFID

BACFID, which stands for Blockchain Crypto Funding Innovation & Development, is the innovation-support arm of SiBAN. Its mission is to support blockchain and crypto innovations by funding projects in Nigeria. BACFID creates avenues for access to funds and supports project development, especially local projects across key industries in Nigeria.

BACE

As we recognize that crypto cannot thrive without proper education, SiBAN maintains an education arm called the Blockchain and Crypto Education Train (BACE Train). BACE Train promotes blockchain and crypto education and awareness by introducing programs and projects that ensure all stakeholders and partners work together to ensure quality blockchain and crypto education across the country.
